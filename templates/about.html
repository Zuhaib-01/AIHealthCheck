About the AI Symptom Checker

Our mission is to provide accessible, AI-driven preliminary health information to help users better understand their symptoms.

Our Technology

We use state-of-the-art machine learning models trained on vast datasets of medical information to analyze reported symptoms. Our goal is to provide a ranked list of potential conditions that could be associated with the symptoms provided by the user.

Important Disclaimer

This tool is for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.
